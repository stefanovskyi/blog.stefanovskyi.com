# Initial Concept
Personal technical blog for Oleksandr Stefanovskyi, focused on bridging the gap between high-level technology strategy and practical engineering execution, with a strong emphasis on Generative AI.

# Product Definition

## Vision
To be a premier resource for senior engineering leaders and technical founders, providing actionable insights on integrating Generative AI into production and aligning technology roadmaps with business objectives. The blog aims to build professional authority and serve as a living portfolio of strategic expertise.

## Target Audience
- Senior engineering leaders and CTOs
- Technical founders and business stakeholders
- Strategic decision-makers integrating AI/ML technologies

## Core Content Pillars
- **Generative AI Strategy:** Insights on production deployment, prompt workflows, domain-specific models, and security rails.
- **Technology Leadership:** Strategic planning, aligning engineering efforts with business goals, and guiding projects from inception to launch.

## Voice and Perspective
- **Strategic & Business-Aligned:** Prioritizes the "why" and "how" for leadership.
- **Experience-Led:** Rooted in real-world projects, microservices system design, and scaling practical applications.

## Primary Call to Action
Encourage high-level professional engagement and strategic technology partnerships.
